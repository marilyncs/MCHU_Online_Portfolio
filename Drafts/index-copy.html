Hello, I'm

Marilyn

Chu

I'm a software engineer & designer based in NYC with a background in art and architecture. My core skills include working with Javascript and the NERDS stack. If I am not online, you can find me exploring the cities or mountains with my golden retriever.



Growing up I've always enjoyed creating art through various mediums, including ceramics, oil painting and pencil drawings. I liked the idea of combining my interests in math and sciences with my creative background in art. This led me to pursue and study architecture at Northeastern University where I recieved my bachelors degree in 2016. I became exposed to the world of web development while working with a team of designers and a software engineer at my last firm. I was curious and took some online courses on my free time and I was instantly hooked which eventually led me to enroll at Grace Hopper Academy in 2019.

I love how quickly I am able to see my designs come to life and the endless possibilities of creation which was hard to come by in architecture. I was once inspired to create beautiful and better physical environments that could be shared with the people around me and now I am able to create products to help enrich and reach the lives of everyday users across the world.

Feel free to reach out via email at marilyn.n.chu@gmail.com or follow me on my social media. Check out my work below or visit my resume for more details about me.
